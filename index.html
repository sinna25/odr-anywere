CONCEPT

LEAP GRAVITY

Through shoes, we will bring about a new way of communication with the earth.

Through shoes, we will make people more aware of the time-gravity link and
make them dream of their connection to higher dimensions.

By making a shoe where the sole-itself is connected to the earth,
with a shape fit for going for a stroll, the burden of walking is lifted.
The transparent soles of the shoes will have you feeling as if you are walking on air.
With a shape that delivers a punch, feel your consciousness flow
through the soles of your shoes.

Pursuing weightlessness and losing consciousness of what's on your feet is not a thing of the future.
By utilizing shape, we want to evolve the consciousness of humans and gravity through comfort.
